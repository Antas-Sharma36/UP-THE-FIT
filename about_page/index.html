About

The goal of this project is to create a website to assist those
who want to reduce their weight. This website uses HTML,CSS and JavaScript.
The website consists of six HTML pages - a BMI calculation page, a home page,
a page for results, a page dedicated to mental health, and an about page.

The first thing will be to login and calculate your BMI. Then based upon your
experience with fitness our platform will suggest a diet plan and a basic
exercise plan. Also, we will also connect the user with our
certified gyms and nutritionists. Further this platform will include
integration with your smart fitness devices like fitness bands, chest bands,
pulse rate counters, smart weight scales.
